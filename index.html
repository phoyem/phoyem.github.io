We are creating Oleic to empower every child on the planet to learn more.

Were on a scientific mission to push the boundaries of AI, developing self-learning programs that can help children learn without following specific curriculums and making learning the way it should be- fun and natural.

Who are we?

Oleic is a small team of gamers, engineers, researchers, teachers and scientists based in Bengaluru, applying the latest in brain-sciences, AI and linguistics to create a self-learning, non-verbal, zero-expertise system from the ground up.

Cognitive Neuroscience

Researches to understand how learning can be associalted with a virtual environment and to understand Computer Human Interactions.

Artificial Intelligence

State-of-the-art research in the latest of AI to create next generation self-learning systems.

Design Language

A new symbol based design language assist learning for those who do not know reading and writing.

Open Source

We look forward to collaborate with extraordinary minds to push our work, which we publish open and free to public.
